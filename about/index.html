Our Story

Tri-City Potlight & Electrical has been wiring Kitchener-area homes since 2009. Here's who we are, what drives us, and why homeowners keep referring us to friends.

Our Story

Started in 2009: Built on One Principle. Do It Right.

Tri-City Potlight & Electrical was founded by James Polak, a licensed master electrician who wanted to build a company that actually explained what it was doing, and stood behind every permit and inspection.

After watching homeowners get burned by unpermitted work that voided their insurance, James built a team that handles every step properly: written quotes, pulled permits, licensed techs, and ESA sign-off on every job.

Today Tri-City runs a team of 6 licensed electricians. We've installed over 5,400 potlights and completed more than 800 panel upgrades across Kitchener, Cambridge, and Waterloo Region.

How We Work

Safety Is Non-Negotiable

Every job is permitted, every installation is inspected by the ESA. We don't cut corners to save time and we never send unlicensed technicians to your home.

Fixed-Price Transparency

You receive a written fixed price before any work begins. The invoice you receive at the end will never exceed the quoted amount. Guaranteed.

Potlight Precision

We've installed 5,400+ potlights across Tri-City homes. Layout planning, spacing ratios, dimmer compatibility; we deliver a finished result that looks custom-designed, not just installed.

Respect for Your Home

We use floor protection, cut drywall cleanly, and restore every surface. You'll see the finished result, not evidence of how we got there.
