How It Works

From Quote to Clean Installation

A straightforward process that keeps you informed and your home protected from start to finish.

1

Free On-Site Quote

We visit your home, assess the job, and provide a written fixed-price quote. No hourly estimates — you know the cost upfront.

2

Permit Pulled

We handle all permits with the municipality and notify the ESA. Your work is done legally and your home insurance remains valid.

3

Clean Installation

Our licensed electricians complete the work precisely, protect your finishes, and leave every room clean and orderly.

4

ESA Inspection

The Electrical Safety Authority inspects and signs off the work. You receive your inspection certificate — peace of mind in one document.
