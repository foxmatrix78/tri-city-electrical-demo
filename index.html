Our Story

Tri-City's Electricians Since 2009

Tri-City Electrical started as a two-person crew out of a service van in Kitchener. Today we're a team of 12 ESA-certified electricians, but our approach hasn't changed: every job gets a permit, every circuit gets tested, and every customer gets a written quote up front.

We've become the go-to electrical contractor across Kitchener, Waterloo, and Cambridge for homeowners who want quality work done once — not twice. From a single pot light to a full panel upgrade with EV charger, we treat every job with the same care.

ESA permit on every job. We never skip the permit. It protects your home, your insurance, and your investment.

No sub-trades, no surprises. Our own certified electricians do the work. You know exactly who's in your home.

We stand behind our work. 2-year warranty on all labour. If something we installed fails, we fix it at no charge.

Dave & Tyler Kowalski Founders, Tri-City Electrical
