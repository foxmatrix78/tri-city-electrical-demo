Our Story

Serving the Tri-City Since 2009

Tri-City Electrical started as a one-truck operation in Kitchener, built on a simple promise: do the work right, treat people fairly, and never cut corners on safety. Fifteen years later, that promise still drives everything we do.

We've grown into a team of licensed master electricians and apprentices — but we still take the time to explain the work, answer your questions, and make sure every job is ESA-certified before we leave. We've wired hundreds of homes and businesses across Kitchener, Waterloo, Cambridge, Guelph, Brantford, Elmira, and New Hamburg.

Family-owned, locally operated. You deal directly with our team — no subcontractors, no outsourcing, no middlemen.

Safety is non-negotiable. Every project meets or exceeds Ontario Electrical Safety Code. ESA inspection on every permit job.

We stand behind our work. If something isn't right, we come back and fix it. No arguments, no charges, no excuses.
