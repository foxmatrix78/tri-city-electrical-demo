  • 100A to 200A service upgrades
  • Sub-panel installation for garages and workshops

  • Fuse panel to breaker panel conversions
  • Certification for home sales and insurance requirements

EV Charger Certified

EV Charger Installation

We install Level 2 home EV chargers for all major electric vehicles. Installation includes the dedicated circuit from your panel, conduit routing to the garage, and the charger mount and connection. We pull the permit and arrange the ESA inspection, with everything handled for you.

  • Compatible with Tesla, Ford, GM, Hyundai, Kia, and all others
  • Dedicated 240V 40A or 50A circuit
  • Indoor and outdoor weatherproof installations
  • ChargePoint, JuiceBox, Grizzl-E, and smart WiFi chargers
  • Permit and ESA inspection included
  • Eligible for federal iZEV grant paperwork
